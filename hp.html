GRYFFINDOR

Godric Gryffindor founded Gryffindor that emphasises the traits of courage as well as "daring, nerve, and chivalry," and thus its members are generally regarded as brave, though sometimes to the point of recklessness.

HUFFLEPUFF

Helga Hufflepuff founded the most inclusive of the four Houses; valuing hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play rather than a particular aptitude in its students.

RAVENCLAW

Rowena Ravenclaw founded this House which prizes learning, wisdom, wit, and intellect in its members. Thus, many Ravenclaws tend to be academically motivated and talented students and shrewd in life.

SLYTHERIN

Founded by Salazar Slytherin, Slytherins tend to be ambitious, shrewd, cunning, strong leaders, and achievement-oriented. They also have highly developed senses of self-preservation.
